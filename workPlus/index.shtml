FAQ

What is work+?

Cleartrip One is an invite-only loyalty program and first of its kind in the Indian travel industry. Along with benefits which frequent fliers will love, every flight or hotel booked on Cleartrip desktop, mobile website, Android or iOS apps, earns you points with your favourite loyalty program.

How many points does each booking earn?

You earn approximately 10% for a hotel booking and about 4% for a flight booking. Points are calculated against the total booking amount, and will be credited your loyalty program partner of choice.

How many points need to be accrued before qualifying for redemption?

All our partners mandate a minimum of 500 points before qualifying for redemption. Vistara need a minimum of 1000.

What are the cancellation benefits available in the program? And how does it work?

  • For every 3 flight journeys (for each passenger oneway is 1 and round-trip is 2 journeys) you earn one cancellation credit for a single flight journey. Eg: If you book a Round-trip for 3 passengers, that’s 6 journeys which will earn you 2 cancellation credits. If you book One-way for 3 passengers, that’s 3 journeys which will earn you 1 cancellation credit.

  • A cancellation credit is equivalent to Airline + Cleartrip Cancellation charges. This amount is credited back into your wallet after the cancellation. For example: You book a Mumbai-Delhi round-trip for 2 passengers, you earn 1 cancellation credit.
